Clarissa Dovey for Schoolmaster!

Running Mate:

Leonora Lesso

Lady Lesso is the Dean of the School for Evil and the best friend of Clarissa Dovey. She would balance out the Good influence from Professor Dovey, keeping everything fair for both schools. These two have known each other for a long time, so they would manage the school well together.

Clarissa Dovey is the Dean of the School for Good and an accomplished fairy godmother, modt well-known for her assistance in Cinderella's story. When she was young, she attended the School for Good and Evil as an Ever of the School for Good.During this time, she became good friends with Leorona Lesso.

She would be a great schoolmaster because she already has experience being in charge of the School for Good. Additionally, she is not biased against the Nevers of the School for Evil as her best friend is from that school.

Campaign Platform

The previous schoolmaster threw the School for Good and Evil into imbalance, causing the Evers to emerge victorious in all stories told under his rule. With Dovey and Lesso in charge, the schools would stabilize. Evers and Nevers would each have a fighting chance at a happy ending.
