Creative Brief

The Problem:

It can be difficult to accurately envision a nail polish look before painting your nails, but at that point you cannot get the polish back if it did not turn out well. This can lead to wasted nail polish and a hesitance to try new color combinations.

The Audience:

The users of this website will primarily be younger people who paint their own nails. The audience may also include people who go to salons to get their nails done. Anyone who wants to have their nail look planned out in advance can benefit from this website.

Competition:

  • Perfect - Virtual Nail Polish Try-On
  • Nail Designs
  • Essie

The first site, Perfect, is very similar to what I want to accomplish, but it does not seem to include a way to share color combinations with other users. The other two websites, Nail Designs and Essie, share nail looks with users, but they do not have a way for users to make their own.

Key Message:

Painting your nails without being able to properly visualize the combination of polishes accurately can result in unsatisfactory nail looks and wasted nail polish. This website helps users avoid these things by providing a way to experiment with different color combinations digitally, compare them with reality, and share the best ones with others.

Call to Action:

Users of the website will improve the look of their nails by taking time to plan out their looks and share the best color combinations with others.

How to Measure Success:

Success for this website would be measured by how many people use the website, which could be tracked based on how many nail looks are shared.
