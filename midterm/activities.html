Things to Do

Children's Museum at Holyoke

The Children's Museum at Holyoke includes a wide variety of educational activities for toddlers, children, and adults. While geared primarily towards children, the exhibits are designed to make it easy for adults to engage in the fun alongside their children. This museum is a great way for children to learn about the world they live in and for adults to see the world through their kids' perspective.

Wistariahurst Museum

Wistariahurst hosts many different events including workshops and concerts. It is a space dedicated to the preservation of Holyoke's history, with the house itself originally being built and owned by the Skinner family in 1874 and later being given to the City of Holyoke in 1959. Wistariahurst is home to beautiful gardens as well as Burlingham Schurr's "Frog Circus" display.

Mount Tom State Reservation

Mount Tom is the highest peak of the Mount Tom Range, reaching an elevation of 1,202 feet. The mountain has several trails for varying skillsets, including skiing in the winter. There are also picnic areas, playgrounds, and fishing spots.
