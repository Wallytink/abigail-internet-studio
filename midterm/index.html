About Holyoke

Holyoke is a city in Massachusetts located north of Springfield on the eastern half of the state. It has a population of about 38,000 people. Holyoke also has strong Irish heritage, with it originally being known as "Ireland Parish" until 1850. The city was built alongside the Holyoke Dam that still stands today.


Interesting Facts

Second largest St Patrick's day parade in the United States

Home of the first community college in Massachusetts

Volleyball was invented at the Holyoke YMCA in 1895
